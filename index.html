Presentation:

After seeing countless artworks on both the S Edition and SPAMM websites I came across the artist LoVid. I have always been a fan of lo-fi 70s-90s digital work and their particular style really resonated with me. The piece I liked in particular was Pink Pop Gulp in which a multicolored animated pattern is mirrored and warped as it moves across the screen. I was created by analog synthesizers and digital composition techniques. Across the pattern floats a transparent, crisp object that clashes with the blurry nature of the background. This contrast allows the viewer to see that this piece was made recently using digital techniques as it would not have been possible to have such a high resolution object without post-2000 digital processing.
